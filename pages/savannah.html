SAVANNAH

Savannah, a city draped in enchantment, where cobblestone streets echo with the tales of Spanish moss-laden oaks, and historic squares resonate with the whispers of centuries past. In this magical haven, the Southern charm dances with ghostly mysteries, weaving a spellbinding tapestry that transcends time, captivating all who stroll beneath the bewitched moonlit archways.

Savannah's squares, known locally as quads, are like open-air galleries showcasing the city's unique blend of history and natural beauty.
